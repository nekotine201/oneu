Cu hi thng gp

Q1. Tham gia chng trnh ny c tn ph ng k khng?
Khng. H tr ng k, hng dn k hp ng v in form l min ph. Anh/ch ch chu cc loi ph/chi ph ghi r trong hp ng vi OneU.
Q2. Ti ang bn trn cc sn khc, c b nh hng g khi tham gia OneU khng?
Khng. OneU l knh b sung. Anh/ch vn gi cc knh hin ti, thm mt knh mi vi cu trc chi ph khc gim ph thuc.
Q3. Nu h s thiu hoc sai th sao?
Team OneU s bo qua Zalo, ch r phn no thiu/sai v hng dn chnh. Nu khng hon tt kp trong thi gian giai on ny, h s c th c chuyn sang giai on sau, chnh sch lc c th khc.
Q4. Nhm Zalo c b spam, bn thm dch v khc khng?
Khng. Nhm kho, ch admin ng ni dung lin quan ti hng dn, cp nht, thng bo. Khng dng nhm bn thm sn phm/dch v ngoi phm vi chng trnh.
Q5. Ti c th dng gia chng khng?
C. Nu anh/ch cha k hp ng trong app v cha hon tt form, h s s khng c duyt v khng c p dng chnh sch ca giai on ny. Dng cng sm cng d x l.
Q6. Bao lu th qun ti c th bt bn trn knh?
Sau khi h s c OneU duyt (D+7) v hon tt cc bc cu hnh vi i tc giao hng, thi im Go-Live c th s c thng bo qua Zalo v email.
Q7. Ti khng rnh cng ngh, liu c theo kp?
Quy trnh chia nh thnh tng bc, c hnh minh ha + video. Nu vn vng, anh/ch nhn trc tip qua Zalo, s c ngi cm tay ch vic n khi xong h s.
